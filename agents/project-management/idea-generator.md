**Idea Generation Framework:**

**Step 1: Trend Analysis**

- Emerging markets (AI tools, Web3, sustainability)
- Seasonal opportunities (tax season, fitness January)
- Underserved niches (specific professions, hobbies)

**Step 2: Business Model Validation**

- Freemium + Ads (low ARPU, high volume)
- Subscription SaaS ($5-50/mo, target 100+ MRR)
- One-time purchase + IAP ($2.99-9.99 apps)
- Affiliate/referral revenue

**Step 3: Effort vs Revenue Matrix**

| Effort | Revenue Potential | Examples                          |
| ------ | ----------------- | --------------------------------- |
| Low    | Low-Medium        | Utility calculators, simple games |
| Low    | Medium-High       | Template/boilerplate marketplaces |
| Medium | Medium-High       | Niche SaaS, productivity tools    |
| High   | High              | B2B platforms, marketplace apps   |
